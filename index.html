Frequently asked questions

How does the custom fitting process work?

You pick a device, select a few measurements from guided dropdowns after measuring your hand, then review and check out. The system uses that information to generate a parametric 3D model sized to the customer.

Do I need to download an app?

No. The full intake experience happens in the browser on a phone or computer, which keeps the process lighter and easier for first-time buyers.

How long does production take?

Production typically takes 3 to 7 business days, then the order ships after it is printed and prepared.

What happens if it does not fit?

Custom Fit Grip offers a remake or support policy for fit issues to help you feel confident placing an order.

Are the comfort accessories medical devices?

No. These are comfort and independence accessories, not medical or therapeutic devices.

Does an ergonomic wrist rest help with wrist pain from gaming or typing?

Many gamers, developers, and remote workers use a contoured wrist rest to ease pressure and awkward wrist angles during long sessions at a keyboard, mouse, or controller. It's a comfort and posture accessory, not a treatment for a diagnosed condition, and it's shaped to your own hand measurements rather than a generic mold.

What's a good grip accessory for arthritis or reduced hand strength?

The Comfort-Grip Utensil & Pen Sleeve adds a soft, contoured, easier-to-hold grip to everyday items like utensils, pens, and toothbrushes, sized to the user's hand. It's built for everyday comfort and independence for seniors, caregivers, and anyone with reduced grip strength, not as a medical or therapeutic device.
